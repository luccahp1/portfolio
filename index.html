the complete map of secrets. last chance to find them yourself.

  • the ghost — this site records your cursor (locally, never sent anywhere). come back tomorrow and watch yourself drift by, labeled "you, 1 day ago". first visit? you got mine instead. summon one anytime: type ghost in the terminal.
  • the pencil — press d, then draw anywhere. c changes ink, z undoes, x erases everything, esc puts it down. your art survives reloads.
  • the terminal — press /. start with help, snoop with cat secrets.txt, finish with sudo hire lucca. (rm -rf goes on your permanent record. permanently.)
  • tell it your name — in the terminal: call me maple. the site greets you by name forever after. lucca.forget() if you regret everything.
  • blueprint mode — ↑↑↓↓←→←→ba. the site shows its bones and measurements. do it twice, it gets chattier.
  • the résumé trick — ctrl+p. the lowercase disappears and paper gets a perfectly serious résumé. the site keeps our secret.
  • the lamp — the cord hanging top-right. pull for lights. it clicks. pull it four times fast and it has opinions.
  • leave & come back — the tab title misses you, and the favicon lies down for a nap while you're gone.
  • the ending changes — speedrun to the bottom and the footer congratulates your world-record pace. read slowly and it thanks you properly.
  • small acknowledgments — copy some text. select all. go idle for a minute. hover the word "eavestroughs". the site notices things, politely, once.
  • the console — open devtools. there's a note waiting, and an api: lucca.ghost(), lucca.lights(), lucca.blueprint(), lucca.forget().
  • for completionists — humans.txt, the 404 page (make up a url), and a note at the top of view-source.

that's all of them. probably. this list is hand-maintained, so if i add a secret and forget to write it down… that's just a secret again.
